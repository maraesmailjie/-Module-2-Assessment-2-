TRAILER

Director: Craig Freimond.

Written by: Craig Freimond; Ronnie Apteker; Robbie Thorpe; Rosalind Butler; Riaad Moosa.

Run Time: 1h 41m

Synopsis: Cassim Caif (Riaad Moosa) is a young Joburg stand-up comedian living in Johannesburg. He has to balance the new marriage, a young child, and caring for his difficult, live-in parents. While Cassim’s is a hit with Johannesburg’s audiences, he yearns to spread his wings, expand and his repertoire. His best friend and agent Yusuf (Joey Rasdien) plans a national tour for Cassim.

Cast: Schalk Bezuidenhout,Joey Rasdien,Riaad Moosa.
